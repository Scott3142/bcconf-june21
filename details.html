Keynote Speakers

[TBC] Striving for Excellence

Colin Jackson

With an outstanding competitive career behind him coupled with his fresh-faced charm and friendly warmth, Colin is the perfect choice as a high level motivational speaker.

Touching on how he took his eye off the ball and didn't win gold when it mattered, Colin combines an inspirational story with life-lessons on how to cope with anxiety and mental health.

Andrew McMillan

Passionate about exemplary customer service, change management and leadership, Andrew McMillan creates an unwavering connection with every audience he addresses as an engaging keynote speaker.

After three decades at John Lewis, he was key to the legendary customer service which now defines its success. Breaking the facts down to a theoretical level, Andrew promotes engagement as a vital differentiator: the whole team has to be brilliant with the customer, not just some, but all of the time.

Andrew also discusses the importance of employee engagement when leading and managing change in organizations.
